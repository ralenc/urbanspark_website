What we do

More than a warehouse.
A supply chain partner.

We handle the complexity of sourcing, stocking and delivering premium e-bike components so your team can focus on what matters — selling, servicing and growing.

Centralised Munich stock
All 38 SKUs held at our Munich warehouse. No drop-shipping, no delays — stock is ready for same-week dispatch across all zones.
Scheduled weekly delivery
Fixed delivery windows per zone — Munich 3x/week with own fleet, regional zones via DPD 3PL. Reliable, predictable, trackable.
Transparent ordering
Simple B2B ordering with live stock visibility, order history and automatic reorder suggestions based on your sales cadence.
Quality-assured sourcing
Direct supply agreements with Bosch, Shimano, SRAM, Fox, Schwalbe and 6 other tier-1 brands. Genuine parts, full warranty.
Regional coverage
Five delivery zones covering Munich, Stuttgart, Nuremberg, Frankfurt and Augsburg — with express dispatch for urgent requirements.
Dedicated account management
A single point of contact for every dealer partner. From onboarding to ongoing forecasting — we keep your supply chain running smoothly.
